Community Guidelines

Last updated: 4 June 2026

enterio is a tool for reimagining real interior spaces with AI — redesigning rooms, adding furniture, changing styles, lighting, floors, and walls. These guidelines keep the app safe and lawful for everyone. They form part of our User Agreement, and using enterio means agreeing to them.

What enterio is for

Use enterio to redesign and visualise interiors: your own home, a space you are working on, or a room you would like to restyle. The app works best with clear photos of real rooms.

Please do not use enterio to

  • upload images you do not own or have permission to use, including others' copyrighted photos or designs;
  • upload photos of identifiable people without their consent, or try to generate images of real, identifiable individuals;
  • create sexual, nude, or otherwise explicit content;
  • create hateful, harassing, threatening, violent, or discriminatory content;
  • create content that is illegal, promotes illegal activity, or depicts the exploitation of children;
  • create misleading or deceptive imagery intended to defraud or impersonate;
  • attempt to bypass safety filters, usage limits, or the app's security.

Your designs are private

enterio has no public feed. The photos you upload and the designs you generate are kept in your own in-app gallery, tied to your anonymous account, and are not shared with other users. You can delete any creation at any time.

A note on AI results

Results are generated automatically by AI and are not reviewed by a person before you see them. They may occasionally be inaccurate or unexpected. They are for inspiration and visualisation only — not professional design, structural, or safety advice.

Images created in enterio are AI-generated. If you share or publish them, please make clear that they are AI-generated, and do not remove or alter any markings or metadata that identify them as AI-created.

Reporting and enforcement

If something in the app produces content that breaks these guidelines, or you have any concern, report it to enterio.app@gmx.at. We review reports promptly and, where appropriate, remove the offending content and warn, suspend, or permanently ban the accounts responsible.

We operate a zero-tolerance policy for illegal content — including any sexual content involving minors — which we remove and, where the law requires, report to the relevant authorities.

Changes

We may update these guidelines as the app evolves. Material changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date above.
